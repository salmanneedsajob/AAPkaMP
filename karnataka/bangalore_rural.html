Ravikrishna Reddy

Constituency: Bangalore Rural

Education: M.Engg

Age: 40

Occupation: Former software professional and noted Kannada writer

Ravikrishna Reddy inspired by the social awakening in his homeland, left his job in the USA and came back to become a political and social activist who has exposed many corruption scandals in Karnataka. He has participated in many debates and has openly expressed his views on several issues that concern the citizens of Karnataka.

As a resident of Anekal taluk he has had the opportunity to travel extensively through Bangalore Rural district. He has been a key proponent of the Indian Against Corruption movement and was an office bearer of the Loksatta party.

The AamAadmi Party, believes Ravikrishna Reddy is an honest, young and capable alternative to the corrupt and unreliable options that was available to the people of Karnataka.
